Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is an Indian international cricketer and a former captain of the Indian national team. He is regarded as one of the best limited-overs batsmen in the world.

  • Most centuries in a single World Cup (5 in 2019)
  • Only player to score three double centuries in ODIs
  • Highest individual score in an ODI match (264 runs)
  • Leading run-scorer in T20 internationals
  • Multiple IPL championships with Mumbai Indians
